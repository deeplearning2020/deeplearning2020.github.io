Curriculum Vitae

Yeahia Sarker has recently completed his bachelor degree in mechatronics engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology. He has been working as a senior research member at Control System Research Group under the supervision of Dr. Sajal K. Das . His research interests includes generative modeling, graph-structured network, remote sensing, and low-level computer vision tasks. He has pubished over 20 peer-reviewed articles in international conferences and journals (12). He holds an erdos number 5 with an h-index 7 . He has a strong passion for algorithm and data structure and putting machine learning research into production pipeline.
