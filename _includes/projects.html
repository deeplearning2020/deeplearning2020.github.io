Convex Optimization For Neural Network Training

A convex-optimization approach has been introduced considering the angular difference of current gradients y(t) and previous gradients y(t-1) with lagrange multiplier. Superior results have been achieved on multiple benchmark datasets including MNIST, CIFAR10, CIFAR100, and Imagenet 2012.
